ABOUT SYNERGY

A family-run gas engineering business built on trust, expertise, and a commitment to doing things right — every time.

Our Story

Family-Run.
Community Focused.
Gas Safe.

Synergy Gas Services was founded with a simple belief: that customers deserve honest, reliable gas engineering without hidden costs or compromises on quality. We're based right here in Newcastle upon Tyne and have been serving homes and businesses across the North East for over 15 years.

As a family-run business, we treat every job — whether it's a small safety check or a full commercial installation — with the same care and attention to detail. Our engineers are fully Gas Safe registered, continuously trained, and proud of the work they do.

No call-out charges. Free estimates. A 5-year guarantee on all pipework. These aren't just policies — they're promises we keep every single day.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2008

Founded in Newcastle

James Thompson founded Synergy Gas Services from his kitchen table with a single van and a commitment to honest, quality workmanship.

2012

Expanded to Commercial Work

After growing our residential client base, we expanded into commercial gas installations for restaurants, offices, and industrial premises.

2015

LPG Specialist Certification

We gained specialist LPG qualifications and began serving rural properties and farms across Northumberland and beyond.

2019

500th Job Completed

A milestone year — we completed our 500th job and introduced our 5-year pipework guarantee, reinforcing our commitment to long-term quality.

Now

Trusted Across the North East

With a full team of Gas Safe engineers and thousands of satisfied customers, Synergy Gas Services continues to grow — always guided by the same values we started with.
